Dr. Hongxing Ye joined the Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Cleveland State University in 2016. He received his B.S. degree in Information and Communication Engineering , in 2007, and M.S. degree in Systems Engineering , in 2011, both from Xi’an Jiaotong University, China, and the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from the Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago in 2016. He was a visiting researcher at Energy Division in Argonne National Laboratory from Sept 2015 to Dec 2015.

Dr. Hongxing Ye's research interests include power and energy system, large-scale optimization, electricity market, renewable integration, and cyber-physical system security. A proposal based on his original research was funded by National Science Foundation in 2015. He received Sigma Xi Research Excellence Award at Illinois Institute of Technology in 2016. He was honored as Outstanding Reviewer for IEEE Transactions on Power Systems and IEEE Transactions on Sustainable Energy in 2015.
